Data on Linguistics Departments in North America

Welcome to the visualization platform for data on linguistics departments across North America.

Degree conferral data is sourced from the IPEDS Data Center. Faculty data is provided by the Bias in Linguistics project. Course requirement data for linguistics BA degrees was collected by analyzing individual department websites during 2023 and 2024.

You can access and download the raw data on our GitHub repository.
